...

Exploring Sofia

Posted on November 10, 2024

Sofia, my hometown, holds a special place in my heart. From its vibrant streets to its rich history, there's always something to discover. Let me show you around!

...

Vitosha Boulevard buzzes with life, lined with cafés, boutiques, and street performers. The golden domes of Alexander Nevsky Cathedral shimmer in the sunlight, a testament to Sofia's rich history. But my favorite escape is the quiet embrace of Borisova Gradina Park, where the rustle of leaves and the chirping of birds transport you far from the city's hustle. Sofia is a blend of the past and the present—a city that always feels like home.

My Favorite Reads

Posted on November 5, 2024

Books have been my constant companions. From science fiction to biographies, here are some of my favorite reads and why I love them.

...

Two books that have profoundly impacted me are George Orwell's 1984 and Animal Farm. 1984 depicts a dystopian world of constant surveillance and control, challenging us to think about freedom and truth. Animal Farm, on the other hand, cleverly uses animals to show how power can corrupt even the noblest of ideals. Both stories are timeless and thought-provoking, offering lessons that stay with you long after reading.
